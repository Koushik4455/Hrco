Corporate Connect

Colleges already created an atmosphere to develop individuals- with character, commitment, conviction, courtesy and courage, who are cultured, and who can shoulder the responsibility of taking the nation forward and making it stronger than ever before. Colleges are putting maximum effort to place the students in the best companies. We appreciate the efforts of the colleges who are striving to give the best employment to their students however in a recent survey done by Acadamians & Corporate leaders it is revealed that maximum colleges are able to place 40% of their students and study even says that core groups live ECE, EEE, MECH, CIVIL, are not getting enough exposure in terms of placements.

Students from respective groups are opting for IT and ITES Jobs for employment After conducting a thorough study of the reports, we have understood that a huge gap exists between corporate and Campus with respect of the above branches. As there is huge skilled manpower requirement for the core sector hence we are glad to introduce our innovative programme Corporate Connect. The Corporate Connect program is designed to enhance the employment opportunities for the students of colleges who are stepping into the corporate world. Our Mission is to provide employment to 60% of the left-out students in colleges and to give employment in their respective fields.
