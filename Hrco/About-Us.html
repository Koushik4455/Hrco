Our Vision

To build an inclusive society and ensure equal opportunities for all regardless of their background, through efforts that include effective policies and actions that promote equal access. The goal is to achieve this by providing employment opportunities to the youth across the nation.

KEY OBJECTIVES

  • Provide skill development opportunities and vocational training to the youth
  • Provide employment opportunities to the youth by creation of jobs, hosting job fairs, organizing off-campus recruitments, etc.
  • Provide rehabilitation by creating human development activities, self-employment creation & bringing social awareness
  • Disaster relief management during extreme crises like natural disasters, pandemic, etc.
  • Drive important campaigns like food distribution, medicine distribution, stationery distribution, etc. at the times of need
  • Focus towards tribal and rural communities, especially towards helping them attain human development activities, teach the importance of hygiene and good health, distribute sanitary napkins to the tribal and rural women and make them aware about the benefits of it
  • Collaborate with other reputed organizations and work towards the overall development of the nation
