Nipuna Human Development Society

Nipuna Human Development Society (NHDS) is a premier Non-profit organization that is working towards building a powerful nation through its diverse employment initiatives and programmes for the youth of the nation. Nipuna was established on 25th March, 2020, on the auspicious day of Chaitra Suddha Padyami, Ugadi (First day of Telugu year). Since its inception, it has been doing ground-breaking work in the field of social services based on the principle “The stronger the youth, the stronger will be the nation.”

Nipuna is working round the clock on various developmental activities that focus on building a better nation for its citizens. It is focusing in the areas of employment, health, and community development to build an inclusive society. The key objective is to empower people by increasing their quality of life, earn a decent income, and gain from the sustainable management of the resources available to them.
