VENDOR CONNECT PROGRAM

Unemployment is one of the major issue facing by our country these days. There are many job opportunities available in the market and on the other hand many unemployed youth are there who are looking for jobs. The companies are spending a lot to recruit the candidates. This is all happening due to the lack of proper approach both from the company side as well as from the candidates side. To overcome this hurdle HRCo introduced a new program and called it as Vendor Connect Program. As a part of it HRCo is making contract with different vendors from all the districts throughout the states. The vendor will be connected to the candidates and through the vendor HRCo will connect the companies to the candidates through the recruitment drives or direct interviews. With this model of approach it will be easy to the companies to recruit good number of candidates.

Benefits of the program:

  • Continuous job openings from the clients. (Both IT and Non IT).
  • Job openings available from the local companies too.
  • Zero consultation charges
  • Free enrollment into our Job Portal ( PAB Jobs) through dedicated Vendor Login.
  • Dedicated resource will be allotted to each and every district.

HRCo was established by group of IT professionals with years of experience in industry. At HRCo, we provide Talent Management Solutions to IT, ITIS, ITES, BPO, and Financial Sectors.
